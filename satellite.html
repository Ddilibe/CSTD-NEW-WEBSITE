Mission Overview

SAR Satellite Mission Objectives

Primary Objectives

  • High-resolution Earth observation capabilities
  • Weather-independent imaging operations
  • Maritime and land surface monitoring
  • Disaster response and management support
  • Agricultural and environmental monitoring

Technical Specifications

  • Resolution: 1-3 meters in stripmap mode
  • Swath width: 20-100 km (mode dependent)
  • Polarization: Dual-pol capability (HH/VV)
  • Mission duration: 3-5 years minimum
  • Data downlink: X-band at 100 Mbps
