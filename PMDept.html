CSTD Project Management Department

Structuring for Success & Efficiency

To provide strategic oversight, guidance, and support for all projects within the Centre for Satellite Technology Development (CSTD), ensuring efficient execution, adherence to timelines and budgets, quality delivery, and alignment with CSTD's vision, mission, and strategic objectives. This department will empower engineers, scientists, and other staff to successfully deliver their projects.
